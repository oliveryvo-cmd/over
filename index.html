// WHAT IS O.V.E.R

We Are Done
Watching It Happen.

O.V.E.R is a worldwide organization founded by Oliver Vo — a 10 year old kid from Ladera Ranch, CA — dedicated to ending the ridicule of disabled kids and empowering them to rise above. Because every disabled kid deserves more than survival. They deserve to thrive.

Not charity. Power.

Other organizations give disabled kids sympathy. O.V.E.R gives them support, community, care packages, and a worldwide army of allies who stand beside them — in their schools, in their neighborhoods, everywhere.

We go where disabled kids are being laughed at and we change the culture. We train schools. We send care. We build a movement so big that ridiculing a disabled kid becomes the most shameful thing anyone can do.

We Come To Your School

The O.V.E.R School Program enters your school and transforms how every student treats disabled kids — permanently. Here's how it works.
