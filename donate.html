WHO IS OLIVER?

// FOUNDER · AGE 10 · LADERA RANCH, CA

Oliver Vo is 10 years old and has Tourette syndrome. He knows what it feels like to be laughed at, mocked, and left out because of something he can't control.

Instead of accepting it, he built O.V.E.R — a worldwide movement to end the ridicule of disabled kids and send them care packages to remind them they matter.

Your donation doesn't go to a corporation. It goes directly to a 10-year-old kid who decided to change the world — and is actually doing it.
