FEATURED KID

⚡
OLIVER VO
// TOURETTE SYNDROME · AGE 10 · LADERA RANCH, CA

"I have Tourette syndrome and I used to get laughed at every day. So I built a worldwide movement to make sure no disabled kid ever has to feel that way again."

Oliver Vo is 10 years old, and he didn't wait for the world to get better — he decided to build it himself. After being mocked for his Tourette syndrome at school, Oliver founded O.V.E.R: Oliver Vo's Empowering Real Disabled Kids. O.V.E.R sends care packages to disabled kids worldwide, runs school programs to end ridicule, and is building an army of allies to stand up for disabled people everywhere. Oliver's goal: to make ridiculing disabled people punishable by law in every country on earth. He is 10 years old. He is just getting started.
