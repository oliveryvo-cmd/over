// WHY THIS MATTERS

THE PROBLEM IS REAL

Disabled kids in America and around the world are legally mocked every single day. Kids with Tourette syndrome, autism, cerebral palsy, Down syndrome, deafness — they walk into school knowing they will be laughed at, imitated, and humiliated. And when they report it, they're told it's "just kids being kids."

It is not "just kids being kids." It is targeted cruelty. It causes depression, anxiety, school avoidance, and in the worst cases, it ends lives. This petition demands that lawmakers treat the ridiculing of disabled people the same way they treat other forms of targeted harassment — with real legal consequences.
