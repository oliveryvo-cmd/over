O.V.E.R ALLY

You don't have to be disabled to stand up for disabled kids. Allies are the kids who refuse to stay silent — who speak up, stand beside, and help change the world.

// WHAT IT MEANS

THE ALLY PLEDGE

01

I WILL NEVER MOCK

I promise I will never make fun of, imitate, or laugh at a disabled person — not in person, not online, not ever.

02

I WILL SPEAK UP

When I see someone ridiculing a disabled kid, I won't stay quiet. I will speak up, step in, or get an adult — even if it's hard.

03

I WILL STAND BESIDE

I will treat disabled kids the same way I want to be treated — as real people who are just as worthy of respect, friendship, and belonging.

04

I WILL HELP GROW THIS

I will tell other kids about O.V.E.R. The more allies we have, the safer the world gets for disabled kids everywhere.
